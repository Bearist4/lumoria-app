The style step gets a new look. The pre-made themes you already know are now grouped into a collapsible up top, and below them sit four new color controls — *Accent*, *Accent text color*, *Background color*, *Text color* — that let you tweak any individual element on a Studio ticket without leaving the funnel. Pick from a curated swatch list or open iOS' native color picker for the exact hex you want. The preview tile labels every recolorable region with a leader pill so you always know what each control affects. Per-element customization is a Premium feature; pre-made themes stay free for everyone.
