The new **Lumiere** template lights up the **Movies** category. Type a film title and Lumiere quietly asks OMDb for the official poster + director, then drops the artwork into a black cinema stub — horizontal or portrait — alongside your cinema, screening time, room, row and seat. The poster fills the right column (or upper portion in portrait) under a deep-black gradient so the title reads as a marquee. Director comes back as a soft sub-line under the movie title; everything stays editable if OMDb's pick isn't quite right. Lumiere ships free, no early-adopter gate, with three knobs in the style step: background color, accent color (the small-caps date / room / seat labels), and text color.
